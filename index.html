ABOUT US

We are a visionary interior and exterior design firm dedicated to creating spaces that are not only visually stunning but also rich in meaning, comfort, and personality. With a deep appreciation for both form and function, we specialize in crafting tailored environments that reflect the unique identity and lifestyle of each client. From private residences to commercial spaces, our work spans a wide range of design styles—including modern minimalism, bohemian charm, rustic warmth, earthy organic textures, luxurious opulence, and timeless traditional elegance.

Our team of passionate designers and architects approaches every project with a refined eye for detail and a strong sense of narrative. We offer end-to-end services—from spatial planning, material curation, lighting design, and custom furniture creation to landscape design, facade detailing, and outdoor styling. Whether it’s a serene, nature-inspired retreat, a bold and expressive modern home, or a heritage-inspired space rooted in tradition, we blend aesthetics with authenticity to deliver designs that are both beautiful and functional.

Our exterior design services encompass everything from contemporary facades and structured landscaping to traditional courtyard layouts and elegant outdoor living areas. On the interior side, we create cohesive, layered environments that feel elevated yet personal—curating textures, colors, and forms that flow naturally and reflect the soul of the space.

At ARS AUREA, design is more than decoration—it is storytelling through space. We combine classical inspiration with contemporary innovation, ensuring every project is a masterpiece of intention, creativity, and craftsmanship. With a client-first philosophy and a deep respect for the art of design, we strive to make every space we touch a timeless expression of beauty, identity, and lifestyle.

TEAM of ARS AUREA

Aurelia Voss

Aurelia Voss is the creative soul behind some of the most opulent interiors in Europe. Known for her ethereal blend of classical motifs and avant-garde elements, she crafts spaces that feel like modern-day cathedrals. Her work often incorporates rare marbles, gold-leaf detailing, and dramatic lighting, making every room a bold expression of luxury and art.

Leif Marten

Leif Marten is a visionary exterior designer whose architecture is deeply rooted in harmony with nature. His structures seem to rise from the earth, integrating raw stone, reclaimed wood, and glass to form timeless silhouettes. Whether designing a clifftop villa or a city pavilion, Leif brings a sense of peace and permanence to every environment he touches.

Seraphina Khouri

Seraphina Khouri transforms interiors into poetic experiences. With a background in fine arts and textile design, she infuses her spaces with softness, layering velvet, silk, and hand-painted murals. Her designs often explore themes of femininity, light, and cultural nostalgia, making her a favorite among collectors and creative minds.

Theo Ardent

A master of structural storytelling, Theo Ardent crafts architectural exteriors that double as sculptural works. With a preference for monumental forms and minimalist detailing, his work balances grandeur with subtlety. Theo’s projects often include integrated water features and sensory materials, engaging both emotion and intellect.

Calista Reign

Calista Reign redefines interior sophistication through her signature “Modern Royalty” style—merging rich color palettes with sleek contemporary finishes. Known for her instinctive spatial flow and refined material curation, she transforms ordinary rooms into regal sanctuaries. Her interiors are favored by luxury hotels and high-end residences across the globe.

Dorian Vale

Dorian Vale is a boundary-pushing architect known for his futuristic facades and kinetic structures. His work lives at the intersection of art, science, and sustainability, often incorporating solar-responsive surfaces and adaptive materials. Dorian’s vision brings a bold, intelligent energy to every skyline he touches.

Eleni Navarro

A master of sensorial design, Eleni Navarro crafts interiors that invite serenity and introspection. Her spaces are built around organic movement, soothing earth tones, and biophilic elements. Every detail—from scent to acoustics—is thoughtfully considered, making her work feel like a retreat from the chaos of modern life.

Cassian Rhys

Cassian Rhys specializes in neoclassical revival, reinterpreting ancient forms with a modern soul. His exteriors feature columns, courtyards, and arcades—reimagined through contemporary materials like glass, bronze, and blackened steel. Cassian’s design language speaks to legacy, permanence, and the enduring beauty of structure.
